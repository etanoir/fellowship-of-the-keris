# Dua Menara Berapi: The Two Volcanic Towers
## Sequel to 'The Fellowship of the Keris'

### Narrative Structure
The story follows three parallel paths:
1. **The Path of the King:** Raden Wijaya, Dewi Anjani, and Tumenggung Batu journeying to Mataram.
2. **The Path of the Forest Guardians:** Cepot and Dawala encountering the Sanghyang Wana and attacking Merapi.
3. **The Path of the Bearer:** Kian Santang and Bagus guided by Si Burik through the volcanic wastes.

---

### Chapter Outline

#### Part I: The Treacherous Path (Krakatau to Mataram)
1. **Chapter 01: Keberangkatan di Fajar Merah (Departure in the Red Dawn)**
   - The five companions leave the volcanic island. A sudden attack by Leak scouts separates Cepot and Dawala from the group in the dense coastal mangroves of Jawa.
   - *LOTR: The Departure of Boromir / The Taming of Sméagol (Hunters chasing)*

2. **Chapter 02: Si Burik di Rawa Bakau (Si Burik in the Mangrove Swamps)**
   - Kian Santang and Bagus are ambushed by Si Burik, a withered creature once a Tuyul bearer of a shadow keris. They capture him and force him to guide them to the landward side of the strait.
   - *LOTR: The Taming of Sméagol*

3. **Chapter 03: Sanghyang Wana di Wanantara (The Forest Guardians of Wanantara)**
   - Cepot and Dawala, lost in the deep forests, encounter **Mbah Jangkung**, an ancient Sanghyang Wana (Banyan spirit). They learn of the corruption of Merapi.
   - *LOTR: Treebeard*

#### Part II: The White Sage and the King
4. **Chapter 04: Pasukan Kuda Kediri (The Horsemen of Kediri)**
   - Raden Wijaya and his companions meet the border guards of the eastern kingdoms, led by **Kanjeng Tumenggung** (Eomer equivalent). They learn of the chaos in Mataram.
   - *LOTR: The Riders of Rohan*

5. **Chapter 05: Dwija Putih (The White Sage)**
   - The Three Hunters encounter a figure in white in the forests of Merapi. It is Eyang Guru Batara, returned from the sacred fire (*Geni Suci*) as the Dwija Putih.
   - *LOTR: The White Rider*

6. **Chapter 06: Balairung di Giri Mataram (The Great Hall of Giri Mataram)**
   - Arrival at the court of **Prabu Jayabaya** (Theoden equivalent), who is under the influence of **Ki Surasenta** (Wormtongue equivalent), a spy of Empu Gandring.
   - *LOTR: The King of the Golden Hall*

#### Part III: The Siege and the Storm
7. **Chapter 07: Pintu Gerbang Malapetaka (The Gate of Doom)**
   - Kian Santang, Bagus, and Si Burik reach the 'Black Gate' of Krakatau's inner realm, but find it impassable. Si Burik suggests a secret path: **Gua Kalajengking** (Shelob's Lair equivalent).
   - *LOTR: The Black Gate is Closed / The Passage of the Marshes*

8. **Chapter 08: Benteng Candi Abang (The Siege of the Red Temple)**
   - The armies of Empu Gandring lay siege to the mountain stronghold of Candi Abang. Raden Wijaya leads the defense.
   - *LOTR: Helm's Deep*

9. **Chapter 09: Amarah Sanghyang Wana (The Wrath of the Forest Guardians)**
   - Mbah Jangkung leads the Sanghyang Wana in a march on Gunung Merapi. They flood the ironworks of Empu Gandring with the waters of the sacred lake.
   - *LOTR: The Road to Isengard / Flotsam and Jetsam*

#### Part IV: The Web of the Shadow
10. **Chapter 10: Gua Kalajengking (The Scorpion's Lair)**
    - Kian Santang is betrayed by Si Burik and attacked by the great spectral scorpion, **Nyi Blorong**'s spawn or a similar beast. Bagus rescues him but Kian is captured by Buta scouts.
    - *LOTR: Shelob's Lair / The Choices of Master Samwise*

---

### Key Mappings
- **The Two Towers:** Krakatau (Raja Iblis) and Merapi (Empu Gandring).
- **Mataram:** Rohan equivalent (The Kingdom of the Plains).
- **Giri Mataram / Candi Abang:** Helm's Deep equivalent.
- **Sanghyang Wana:** Ents equivalent (Banyan tree spirits).
- **Mbah Jangkung:** Treebeard equivalent.
- **Si Burik:** Gollum equivalent (corrupted former Tuyul).
- **Dwija Putih:** Gandalf the White (Eyang Guru Batara).
- **Ki Surasenta:** Gríma Wormtongue.

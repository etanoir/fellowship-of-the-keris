## Summary of Findings

### PASSED (No Issues)
1. **Dalang Framing Structure** -- All 10 chapters open and close with proper Sundanese/English italic Dalang framing, separated by horizontal rules.
2. **Narrative Continuity** -- All 9 chapter transitions correctly bridge to the next narrative path. The Dalang closing text precisely foreshadows the next chapter.
3. **Mbah Jangkung's Voice** -- Geological slow speech with ellipsis separation is perfectly consistent.
4. **Dwija Putih's Voice** -- Sacred fire imagery present in all three of his chapters.
5. **Raden Wijaya's Krama Inggil** -- "Duh Gusti" present in all King-path chapters.

### PASSED WITH NOTES
6. **Wayang Convention Maturity** -- Chapters 1-3 use simplified Dalang framing (no explicit Gunungan position); chapters 4-10 use full formal wayang convention. May be intentional design.
7. **Color-as-Opening** -- 8 of 10 chapters open with explicit color. Ch3 (green implied, not stated) and Ch10 (tactile metaphor instead) deviate.
8. **Section Naming** -- Inconsistent between letter (A-E) and Roman numeral (I-VII) schemes across chapters.

### FAILED (Action Required)
9. **"gollum" missing from Chapter 2** -- Si Burik's signature verbal tic "gollum...gollum" appears zero times in his introduction chapter. This is a continuity error that should be corrected. The tic appears 23 times in Ch7 and 11 times in Ch10.
10. **"Jimat-ku" missing from Chapter 7** -- Si Burik's possessive reference to the talisman is absent from the Gate of Doom chapter.
11. **"Rahayu" severely underrepresented** -- Only 3 occurrences total. This traditional Sundanese blessing should appear more frequently across the manuscript.
12. **"Inggih" missing from Chapter 5** -- Raden Wijaya's Krama Inggil affirmation is absent from the Dwija Putih chapter despite his prominence.
13. **"Mulah" missing from Chapter 9** -- Cepot's signature exclamation is absent from his second major chapter.

### Priority Recommendations

1. **HIGH**: Add "gollum...gollum" verbal tic to Si Burik's dialogue in Chapter 2. At minimum 5-8 occurrences during the stalking, encounter, and oath-binding scenes.
2. **MEDIUM**: Add "Jimat-ku" to Si Burik's dialogue in Chapter 7.
3. **MEDIUM**: Sprinkle "Rahayu" into more chapters as greetings/blessings, particularly in chapters 1, 3, 4, 6, 8.
4. **LOW**: Add "Inggih" to Raden Wijaya's dialogue in Chapter 5.
5. **LOW**: Add "Mulah" to Cepot's dialogue in Chapter 9.
6. **LOW**: Consider adding explicit color to the opening lines of Chapters 3 and 10.
7. **LOW**: Consider standardizing section naming (all Roman numerals or all letters).
